A navigation game for undergrads interested in neuro

Thresholds

You are studying something that touches the brain, whether neuroscience, biomedical engineering, electrical engineering, computer science, or something adjacent, and you want to do something that matters in this field. Fantastic!

Neuro is exciting. Some parts can be tricky to break into. Some companies rarely hire early-career people. And in a world where AI can generate a resume in thirty seconds, being a real person who has done things and built real relationships is more of a differentiator than it has ever been.

Eleven scenarios in three acts. No assumed career plan. Choose your own adventure to move past thresholds into neuro.
