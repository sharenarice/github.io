A navigation game for grad students

Growing Into the Field

You are in a graduate program, whether a master's or a PhD, in a field connected to neuroscience, biomedical engineering, or something adjacent. You care about doing work that matters. You are also navigating a system that was not designed with your interests as the primary consideration. In a world where AI can generate a resume in thirty seconds, being a real person who has done things and built real relationships is more of a differentiator than it has ever been.

This game covers structural patterns that shape your training and your options, whether or not you have names for them yet. Twelve scenarios. Three acts. Some of what comes up will be familiar. Some will be new. Choose your own adventure.
