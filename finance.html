Additional Tools
Extras & advanced modeling
Tier 1
Backdoor Roth IRA tracker

At your MAGI ($136,817 with max 401(k)), your $7,500 traditional IRA contribution is non-deductible AND gains are taxed as ordinary income on withdrawal. A backdoor Roth conversion gets you tax-free growth for the same contribution.

Triggers pro-rata rule if >0
Tier 1
Tax-loss harvesting calculator

Harvest realized losses against capital gains, plus up to $3,000/year against ordinary income. Wash-sale rule blocks buying substantially identical securities within 30 days before or after the sale.

Tier 1
Cash flow & emergency fund

How many months of COL your liquid cash covers, and the opportunity cost of over-funding (cash at 4.5% HYSA vs 11% expected market return).

Tier 3
FI year sensitivity matrix

How your FI year shifts across return rate × SWR combinations. Other assumptions from the Assumptions panel.

Tier 3
Monte Carlo FI projection

1,000 simulated scenarios using historical S&P return distributions (approximate mean 10%, standard deviation 16%). Produces probability bands around your FI year rather than a single point estimate.

Tier 3
Portfolio allocation breakdown

Account-by-account balances for asset-location optimization. Bonds in tax-deferred, growth in taxable/Roth is the general pattern.

Tier 2
Neuradaptive full-time transition modeler
Simplified

Compare current compensation (Sanmai + contracts) against hypothetical full-time Neuradaptive offer. Equity modeled as expected value = shares × strike-discounted FMV × exit probability.

Tier 2
Bay Area rent vs buy
Simplified

Current rent is structurally excellent. This models the cost of trading it for ownership. Does not include HOA, appliance replacements, or homeowners insurance.

Tier 2
529 education fund
Placeholder · activate when relevant

Superfunding exception lets you contribute 5 years of gift exclusion at once ($95K single in 2026) without using lifetime estate exemption. California does not offer a state income tax deduction on 529 contributions. Inputs below model a standard annual contribution strategy.

Tier 2
Post-FI health insurance (ACA)
Simplified

After leaving W-2 employment, ACA premium tax credits phase out above 400% FPL. Keeping MAGI under ~$60K single at FI could save $8-15K/year in unsubsidized premiums. This interacts with Roth conversion strategy.

Tier 4
State relocation modeler
Planned feature

California's 9.3%+ marginal rate, 1% MHSA over $1M, and 13.3% top rate are uniquely costly. Moving to Texas, Washington, Florida, or Nevada at or near FI eliminates state income tax entirely. A full implementation would model: moving costs, establishment of domicile requirements (183-day rule for most states, CA's aggressive retention rules), Prop 13 loss if selling CA property, and compounded tax savings over 20+ years.

Tier 4
Social Security projection
Planned feature

At 35+ years from full retirement age (67), Social Security modeling has high uncertainty. Near-term relevance is minimal. A future implementation would estimate PIA (Primary Insurance Amount) from your earnings record, factor in the 2034 trust fund depletion scenario (currently projected to reduce benefits to 77% of scheduled), and model claim-age tradeoffs (62 vs 67 vs 70).

Tier 4
Estate planning & beneficiaries
Planned feature

Federal estate exemption is $15M per person in 2026 (OBBBA). California has no state estate tax. A future implementation would track: beneficiary designations across all accounts, revocable living trust structure, healthcare directive + POA documentation status, and step-up basis implications for taxable brokerage at death.
